**Swallowtail Butterflies** are large, colorful butterflies named for the tail-like extensions on their hindwings, which resemble the tails of swallow birds. There are over 550 species of swallowtails.

*   **Region:** Worldwide, except Antarctica.
*   **Diet:** Nectar from various flowers.
*   **Fun Fact:** Some swallowtail caterpillars mimic bird droppings to avoid being eaten.

The **Painted Lady** is known for its orange and black wings with white spots. It is found on every continent except Antarctica and South America.

*   **Region:** Worldwide.
*   **Diet:** Thistle, aster, and other flowers.
*   **Fun Fact:** They are fast fliers and can travel up to 100 miles in a day during migration.

The **Monarch Butterfly** is perhaps the most well-known butterfly in North America. They are famous for their long-distance migration, traveling thousands of miles to overwinter in Mexico.

*   **Region:** North, Central, and South America.
*   **Diet:** Nectar from milkweeds (larvae eat milkweed leaves).
*   **Fun Fact:** Monarchs are poisonous to predators due to the toxins from the milkweed they ate as caterpillars.
